What is Wio Business?
Your 30-second elevator pitch
Wio Business is the UAE's leading digital business banking app — fully licensed by the Central Bank of the UAE, headquartered in Abu Dhabi, and backed by ADQ, Alpha Dhabi, e&, and First Abu Dhabi Bank. It gives SMEs and restaurants a complete financial operating platform: banking, payroll, credit, invoicing, expense management, and more — all in one app.

Top 8 Features to Highlight
Most relevant for F&B restaurant owners
  • Business Credit Card — Free for life, up to AED 250K limit, 2% cashback on all spend (up to AED 2,000/month), 60 days interest-free. Apply in-app, approval in 3 days.
  • WPS Payroll — Pay all staff salaries in a few clicks, MOHRE-compliant, with real-time validation and salary slips generated automatically.
  • Virtual Cards — Create unlimited virtual cards for staff or vendors (e.g., "Kitchen Supplies card"). Set spending limits by category. Add to Apple Pay or Google Pay.
  • Multi-currency Accounts — Open accounts in AED, USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, CHF instantly. Guaranteed AED/USD rate of 3.673 every day.
  • Saving Spaces — Earn up to 3.50% p.a. on Fixed Saving Spaces (Grow plan). Park surplus cash and earn while you wait.
  • Business Loans — Borrow up to AED 3,000,000 for expansion or inventory. 100% digital, flexible terms of 6–48 months, 1% processing fee.
  • Invoicing & Accounting — Create invoices in-app, send payment links, integrate with Zoho, Xero, Wafeq, or Fiskl automatically.
  • Multi-user Access — Add up to 10 team members (Grow plan) with role-based permissions. Managers can prep payments, owners approve via biometrics.
